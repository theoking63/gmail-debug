About DevStatus

DevStatus was founded in 2022 by a team of DevOps engineers who experienced the June 2022 GitHub outage firsthand. After spending hours debugging what appeared to be local authentication issues, only to discover it was a service-wide problem, we realized developers needed better visibility into external service health.

Today, DevStatus monitors over 15,000 developer integrations across GitHub, npm, AWS, Docker Hub, and other critical services. Our intelligent monitoring detects authentication anomalies, API rate limiting issues, and service degradations before they impact your workflows.

How DevStatus Works

DevStatus uses distributed monitoring nodes to continuously test authentication flows, API responses, and service availability across major developer platforms. When we detect anomalies:

  • Real-time alerts are sent to affected developers
  • Detailed technical analysis helps distinguish service issues from local problems
  • Resolution guidance prevents time wasted on unnecessary debugging
  • Historical data helps predict and prevent future issues
